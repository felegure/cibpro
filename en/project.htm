.

One of the highlighted challenges for the subregion at the Ghana meeting was managing the logistics for this strategy.

3) The coordinated informed buying (CIB) system is an integral part of this strategy in the subregion.

Using CIB, the personnel responsible for purchasing and supply ing of reproductive health commodities will be able to share information with their counterparts in the subregion about the prices of drugs , the suppliers, and the quality , as well as of other relative data needed for purchases.

The sharing of information will allow :

•  numerous advantages for purchasing systems

•  harmonization of the regulations

•  better management of the supply chain

•  reduction of wastes and losses .

II. Justification

Why coordinated informed buying system

While sharing information on the prices of the products and purchases, as well as the names of the different used suppliers, countries will be able to more efficiently increase the efficiency of their purchases of the essential products of reproductive health and medicines products , such as the antiretrovirals (ARV s ) and other essential products.

CIB is only a way to share information. Each of the member countries will need to finance, organize , and execute their purchases.
